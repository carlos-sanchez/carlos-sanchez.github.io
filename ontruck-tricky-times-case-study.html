Ontruck Case Study: Solving the Tricky Times Problem

Context

Ontruck is a service that allows companies who have road freight shipping needs to easily book and track shipments online. We refer to these companies as shippers.

Shippers can book shipments with as many pickups and dropoffs as they need. When booking a shipment, we ask them to provide a time window for each stop. Time windows of zero minutes are allowed because pickup/dropoff points often provide shippers specific appointment times.
