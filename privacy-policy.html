Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Who I am

This website, carlossanchez.me, is the personal portfolio of Carlos Sánchez. For anything related to this policy, you can reach me at hi@carlossanchez.me.

What data I collect

None. This site does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or contact forms. I do not collect, store, or process any personal data from visitors.

Hosting

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub, Inc. Like most hosting providers, GitHub may log technical information from visitors (such as IP addresses) for security and operational purposes. This is described in the GitHub Privacy Statement. I have no access to or control over these logs.

Fonts

This site loads the Inter typeface from Google Fonts. When your browser requests the font files, Google receives your IP address as part of that request. You can learn more in the Google Fonts privacy FAQ.

External links

This site links to external websites and services (such as X, LinkedIn, and Medium). Once you leave this site, their own privacy policies apply.

Email

If you email me, I will use your address only to reply to you. I do not add addresses to mailing lists or share them with anyone.

Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have rights over your personal data, including access, rectification, and erasure. Since this site does not collect personal data, these rights mainly apply to email correspondence. To exercise them, write to hi@carlossanchez.me.

Changes to this policy

If this site's setup changes in a way that affects your privacy, this page will be updated and the date above revised.
