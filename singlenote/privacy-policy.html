Singlenote

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Singlenote stores your note exclusively on your device — your note content never leaves it. We collect only the minimal data needed to improve the app: anonymous usage analytics and crash reports.

1. Information we collect

We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of the following:

  • Your note content
  • Your name, email address, or any contact information

All note content — text, checklists, links, and images — is stored exclusively in your device's local app storage and never leaves your device.

2. Analytics and crash reporting

We collect anonymous usage analytics and crash reports to understand how the app is used and to improve stability. This data may include:

  • Anonymous usage events (such as features accessed and session duration)
  • Crash logs and diagnostic information
  • Device type, OS version, and country-level location derived from IP address

Analytics data does not include the content of your note and cannot be used to identify you personally. We will update this section with the specific analytics tools used as they are integrated.

3. Photo library access

Singlenote may request access to your photo library so you can insert images into your note. Images you choose to insert are copied to the app's local storage on your device. They are not uploaded to any server.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

We plan to offer optional paid features processed through Apple App Store and Google Play. Purchases are managed through RevenueCat, a third-party subscription management platform. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat may collect:

  • A randomly generated, anonymous user identifier
  • Purchase receipts and transaction history
  • Device type and OS version, for fraud prevention

RevenueCat does not receive the content of your note. For details on how RevenueCat handles data, see their Privacy Policy.

Purchases are also subject to Apple's and Google's privacy policies depending on your device.

5. Third-party services

Beyond analytics, crash reporting, and RevenueCat (used only during a purchase), Singlenote is not integrated with advertising networks, additional analytics providers, or social platforms.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your note content exists only on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app or use the in-app "Delete note" function. Anonymous analytics and crash report data is retained by the analytics provider according to their own retention policies.

7. Children's privacy

Singlenote is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as we add new features. If we make material changes — for example, if we introduce server-side features or additional data collection — we will update the "Last updated" date and notify you within the app. Continued use of Singlenote after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hi@carlossanchez.me.
